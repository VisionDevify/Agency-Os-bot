# Team Invite Packet

Use these messages when inviting real teammates into Agency OS. Do not include internal admin notes, secrets, Railway links, database details, proxy credentials, platform passwords, or verification codes.

## Chatter Invite

```text
Agency OS invite for Chatter
1. Open @FortunaSolstice_Bot in Telegram.
2. Press /start.
3. Choose your language, country, timezone, and 12h/24h time format.
4. Wait for approval. You will see Access pending approval until an owner/admin approves you.
5. After approval, your Chatter home will show: My Models, My Opportunities, My Tasks, Availability, and Help.
Do not send passwords or verification codes in onboarding.
```

What they should do first after approval:

1. Open Availability and set whether they are on shift.
2. Open My Models to confirm assignments.
3. Open My Opportunities and My Tasks.
4. Use Help if anything is unclear.

## Pending Approval

Pending approval means the user has registered with the bot but cannot access agency work yet. Owner/Admin should approve only known teammates, then assign the correct role, timezone, availability expectation, and model/team assignments.

## Owner/Admin Checklist

1. Ask the teammate to press `/start`.
2. Confirm they completed language, country, timezone, and time format setup.
3. Open Users or Team Activation.
4. Approve the known user.
5. Assign the correct role.
6. Assign model/team membership if needed.
7. Ask them to open their home screen and Help Center.
